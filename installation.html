Installation Guide

Our verandas and glass rooms are supplied with detailed installation instructions. Follow these steps:

  1. Prepare your site with a level base (concrete or paving recommended).
  2. Assemble the frame using the provided hardware, starting with the main supports.
  3. Attach the roof panels or glass sections securely.
  4. Check alignment and fasten all connections.
  5. Contact our online support for any assistance: info@verandasandcarports.com.

Tools required: Drill, level, wrench. Always wear safety gear.
