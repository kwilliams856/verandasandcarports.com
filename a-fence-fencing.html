A-Fence Aluminium Fencing

A-Fence is an A-Rated architectural aluminium modular fencing system designed for residential and commercial properties. Combining beauty with practicality, this corrosion-resistant solution offers near-zero maintenance, a 10-year guarantee, and a 60-year life expectancy.

Overview

A-Fence revolutionises perimeter fencing with its lightweight yet strong aluminium construction. Achieving an A2-s1, d0 fire rating, it’s non-combustible, making it a safe choice for future-proofing your property. With a maximum span of 1.8m between posts, it’s ideal for diverse settings.

  • Non-combustible: A2-s1, d0 fire rating for safety.
  • Durability: 60-year life expectancy.
  • Low Maintenance: Minimal cleaning required.
  • Customizable: Adaptable to any design need.

Design Options

Available in Anthracite Grey Textured (RAL 7016) and Jet Black Textured (RAL 9005), A-Fence offers five profile options: 50mm, 100mm, 150mm, and interlocking boards (120mm or 240mm). Create bespoke designs with spaced or closed slats for privacy or aesthetic appeal.

  • Profiles: 50mm, 100mm, 150mm, 120mm, 240mm interlocking.
  • Colors: Anthracite Grey and Jet Black as standard, bespoke options available.
  • Versatility: Spaced or closed configurations.

Installation & Accessories

Installation is quick and simple with few components. Concrete post adapters integrate seamlessly with existing posts, while retaining clips secure panels against movement. All necessary fixings are included for a hassle-free setup.

  • Easy Setup: Minimal components required.
  • Adapters: Compatible with concrete posts.
  • Secure: Retaining clips included.
