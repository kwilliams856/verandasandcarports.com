Verandas

Enhance your outdoor living with our durable verandas, crafted for the UK climate. Enjoy affordable, functional, and stylish solutions with a 10-year guarantee and over 60 years of life expectancy.

Classic Veranda

A timeless design featuring elegant lines, our Classic Veranda offers shade and shelter with customizable lengths and a sturdy aluminium frame. Perfect for period properties and traditional homes.

  • Features: Customizable length, elegant aluminium frame, weather-resistant, timeless look.
  • Benefits: Enhances heritage aesthetics, easy to install, durable construction, low maintenance.

Contemporary Veranda

Our Contemporary Veranda boasts sleek, minimalist lines, ideal for modern homes. With a projection up to 6 meters and unlimited length, it integrates seamlessly into any outdoor space.

  • Features: Sleek design, spans up to 6 meters, unlimited length, modern aesthetic.
  • Benefits: Complements modern architecture, versatile fit, quick installation, enhances property value.

Glass Veranda

Featuring unobstructed garden views and ample natural light, our Glass Veranda creates a relaxed atmosphere. Customizable glass panels offer privacy without sacrificing elegance.

  • Features: Glass panels, natural light, customizable privacy, elegant design.
  • Benefits: Maximizes light, enhances outdoor enjoyment, durable, stylish addition.

Pergola Veranda

Combining pergola charm with veranda practicality, our Pergola Veranda features a louvered roof system to control sunlight. Perfect for year-round outdoor living.

  • Features: Louvered roof, adjustable sunlight, aluminium frame, versatile design.
  • Benefits: Customizable shade, durable structure, enhances garden space, all-weather use.
