A-Fence Aluminium Fencing

Discover A-Fence, a stylish and practical aluminium fencing solution designed for residential and commercial properties. With a 10-year guarantee and a 60-year life expectancy, it offers unmatched durability and low maintenance.

Overview

A-Fence is an A-Rated architectural aluminium modular fencing system that combines beauty with cost-effectiveness. Manufactured from corrosion-resistant aluminium, it requires minimal maintenance and provides a robust, fire-safe installation. Ideal for future-proofing your perimeter fencing.

  • Key Features: Non-combustible (A2-s1, d0 fire rating), lightweight yet strong, customizable designs, 1.8m max span between posts.
  • Benefits: Long-lasting (60 years), low maintenance, enhances property appeal, safe and secure.

Design Options

Available in Anthracite Grey Textured (RAL 7016) and Jet Black Textured (RAL 9005), A-Fence offers five profile options: 50mm, 100mm, 150mm, and interlocking boards (120mm or 240mm). Create bespoke designs with spaced or closed slats for privacy or aesthetic appeal.

  • Features: Five profile sizes, two standard colors, customizable spacing.
  • Benefits: Versatile for any property, easy to tailor, enhances visual harmony.

Installation & Accessories

Installation is quick and easy with few components needed. Concrete post adapters integrate seamlessly with existing posts, ensuring durability. Retaining clips secure panels, preventing movement.

  • Features: Easy installation, concrete post adapters, retaining clips included.
  • Benefits: Hassle-free upgrades, weather-resistant, long-term reliability.
